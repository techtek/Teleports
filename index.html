The Teleports project brings you the most popular, useful, fun and rewarding DAPP websites from the Steem Blockchain, and teleports them into Android apps. The released apps are compatible with Android phones, tablets & tv's. To use some of the Teleports apps you need to have a free Steem account.

The released Teleports apps are free to try, but if you like and use them on a regular base, and want to help the further development, then please donate 2 or more STEEM with Fundition, or send a transfer to @teleports. and you then are free to use them as often as you want.
